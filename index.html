About Oncology

Oncology is the branch of medicine focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. It includes medical, surgical, and radiation oncology. Specialists study tumor biology, develop therapies like chemotherapy and immunotherapy, and provide patient care, aiming to improve survival rates and quality of life.

Medical Team

Professional staff are trained individuals with specialized knowledge who perform skilled roles in an organization. They maintain professionalism, follow ethical standards, ensure quality work, collaborate effectively, and contribute to achieving organizational goals.

Modern Equipments

Modern equipment refers to advanced tools and technology designed to improve efficiency, accuracy, and productivity. They support innovation, enhance performance, reduce effort, and ensure better outcomes across industries and professional environments.

Our Specialities

he specialized departments at Orchid Medical Centre deliver cutting-edge care by combining unmatched expertise with cutting-edge technology in a broad scope of clinical categories.

Pediatric Oncology

Pediatric Oncology is a specialized branch of oncology that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of cancer in children.

Hemato-oncology

Hemato-oncology is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of blood-related cancers, such as leukemia.

Gynecologic Oncology

Gynecologic Oncology is a medical specialty focused on the diagnosis and treatment of cancers of the female reproductive system.

Neuro-oncology

Neuro-oncology is a subspecialty of oncology that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of cancers affecting the brain and nervous system.

Oncology Team

An Oncology team includes doctors, nurses, surgeons, radiologists, and support staff who collaborate to diagnose, treat, and care for cancer patients, ensuring comprehensive and coordinated healthcare services.
