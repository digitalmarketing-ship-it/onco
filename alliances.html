Orchid Samagati Cancer Centre, a state-of-the-art hospital located in the heart of Ranchi, Jharkhand, is comprehensive oncology hospital providing advanced cancer diagnosis, chemotherapy, immunotherapy, radiation therapy, surgical oncology, and multidisciplinary cancer care under one roof.
